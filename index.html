Services

Our clinic is a full-service pet clinic. We provide specialized pet care services at very reasonable rates. Our veterinarians and veterinary technicians have years of experience to provide your family pet with the best quality services in the area.

We cater to all shelters, rescue groups and organizations.

    Our clinic services include:
  • Pet exams
  • Spaying and neutering
  • Small animal medicine and surgery
  • Ultrasound technology
  • Therapy laser (cold laser therapy)
  • Surgical laser
  • Direct digital x-ray
  • Health certificates
